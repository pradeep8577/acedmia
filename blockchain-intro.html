About this course

An "Introduction to Blockchain" course provides foundational knowledge about blockchain technology, its principles, and its applications. This course is typically designed for individuals who are new to blockchain and want to understand its fundamental concepts. The course starts by explaining what a blockchain is. It covers the foundational concepts, including blocks, transactions, distributed ledger technology, and the chronological chain of blocks. Upon completing this introductory blockchain course, students should have a solid understanding of the fundamental concepts, technology, and potential applications of blockchain. They will be prepared to explore more advanced blockchain topics, consider blockchain-related career paths, or apply blockchain knowledge to various industries and sectors.
